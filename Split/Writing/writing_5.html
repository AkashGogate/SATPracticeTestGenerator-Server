Writing Passage

The growth of online education has been exponential in recent years, fundamentally changing how students and teachers interact. Online platforms enable students to learn from anywhere, offering flexibility that traditional classrooms cannot provide. However, challenges such as ensuring student engagement and maintaining quality education remain significant hurdles. The rapid adoption of technology in education has also raised concerns about the digital divide, as not all students have equal access to the necessary tools and resources1.
